Our Story

From Passion to
Perfection

At Café Aura, coffee isn't just a drink — it's a passion, a ritual, and an experience. Founded in 1944, our mission has always been simple: create exceptional coffee that people can truly enjoy, share, and savor every day.

We source our beans directly from smallholder farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil, and the Philippines, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices.
